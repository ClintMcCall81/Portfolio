Future of Information Design Jam

Description

This was designed as part of a Information Design Jam based on the future of information Our team worked 2 hours on research and 4 hours on design. For the first 2 hours we tried needed to decide on a time arc, because it was the future of information we ended up picking 30 years in the future. We then conducted our research, researched the challenges and opportunities, and pain points, then designed senerios based on our results. We then used the "How Might We" question to determine our final idea. Our voted idea was "How can we be consistent and offer the same treatment and convey the same, accurate and up to date information to all users?" We then needed to figure out how we could answer that question and brainstormed solutions. We organized the solutions and then compared them on an axis chart (High Impact, Low Effort, High Effort, Low Impact). After this we designed the final result through Adobe XD
